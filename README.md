# Problem Statement 
- Smart Case Management & Justice System Optimization
Brief Description:
The justice system faces significant delays due to case backlogs, inefficient processes, and limited
resource management. This problem focuses on leveraging technology to streamline case
management, automate routine tasks, assist in legal research, and improve dispute resolution
mechanisms. The goal is to enhance efficiency, reduce delays, and ensure timely delivery of
justice.

- Expected Output:
A digital case management system for courts and legal professionals
Automated scheduling and tracking of case progress
AI-assisted legal research and document analysis
Workflow automation for routine judicial processes
Online dispute resolution and virtual hearing support
Dashboard for monitoring case status and backlog analytics
Integration with existing legal and court systems
Secure data management and confidentiality handling
